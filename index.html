About MITN - Lansing

Strengthening Michigan's technology community through connection, education, and collaboration

Our Mission

The Michigan Technology Network (MITN) is a statewide organization based in Greater Lansing, dedicated to enhancing the development of tech professionals through educational and networking events with a strong focus on community building.

We believe that by connecting talented individuals and providing opportunities for growth, we can strengthen the entire technology ecosystem in Michigan.

[Mission Image Placeholder]
[Community Image Placeholder]

What We Do

MITN organizes regular networking events, educational workshops, and collaborative sessions that bring together professionals from across the technology spectrum. From seasoned developers to emerging talent, our community welcomes everyone.

Our annual Fellowship program provides $2,500 awards to support technology-based educational initiatives, helping to grow the next generation of tech leaders in Michigan.
